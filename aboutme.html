SIDE SCROLLER

"Starfish Adventure" is a side scroller game that I made with a partner in Scratch. Players use the space bar to make the starfish jump. They should collect seashells and avoid sharks and fish.

CIRCUIT THAT TURNS ON AN LED

This is the first robotics project we made. We used an Arduino, Board of Education shield, breadboard, jumper wires, resistor, LEDs, and a button. I learned which way the power runs and how a circuit has to be compelete. I also learned how everything has to line up in order for all the components to work and how important the positioning of the wires is.
